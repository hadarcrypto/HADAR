HADAR Crypto Project Road Map

Launch Phase - November 30, 2022

Token Launch: Introduction of the HADAR token (HDR) to the market. A total of 1 billion HDR tokens were issued at a price of $0.03 each.

Token Distribution: 500 million tokens allocated to the founders, with the remaining 500 million tokens locked forever in a phantom wallet, ensuring no further issuance of HDR.

Year 1 (2023)

Market Introduction: Focused on building awareness and adoption of HDR in the crypto community.

Strategic Partnerships: Establishing connections with key industry players to enhance the HADAR ecosystem's reach and capabilities.

Year 2 (2024)

Alpha Product Development: Initiating the creation of alpha crypto products and trading tools within the HADAR platform, leveraging Web 3.0 technologies.

Platform Development: Starting the development of the HADAR platform, a Web 3.0 building platform for creating crypto products and enabling user interaction.

Year 3 (2025)

Alpha Platform Release: Launching the alpha version of the HADAR platform, showcasing initial functionalities and inviting user feedback for improvements.

Community Building: Expanding the HADAR community through engagements, feedback loops, and beta testing opportunities.

Year 4 (2026)

Product Expansion: Introducing additional features and products based on community feedback and technological advancements in the crypto space.

Token Growth Strategy: Implementing strategies to increase the value of HDR tokens with the goal of reaching a $2 price point, in alignment with market performance as indicated on CoinMarketCap.

Long-Term Vision

Sustainability and Innovation: Commitment to continuously exploring new technologies and trends in the crypto and Web 3.0 space to keep the HADAR platform at the forefront of innovation.

Global Adoption: Aiming for widespread adoption of the HADAR platform and HDR tokens by offering unique, user-friendly, and value-driven crypto products and services.

Note: The HADAR project is marked by its innovative approach to the crypto market, with a finite supply of HDR tokens to ensure scarcity and value appreciation. The roadmap reflects our commitment to transparency, user engagement, and the development of cutting-edge technology to empower users to create and interact with crypto products.

This roadmap serves as a strategic outline of HADAR's journey, highlighting past achievements and outlining the path toward future milestones and innovations in the crypto and Web 3.0 ecosystem.
